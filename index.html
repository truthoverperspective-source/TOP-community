About Dr. Rivera

Therapy that feels grounded, collaborative, and deeply human.

Therapy that feels grounded, collaborative, and deeply human.

I’m Dr. Elena Rivera, LPC, a licensed professional counselor offering therapy in Niantic, CT. My work is rooted in the belief that healing often begins when you feel truly heard—without pressure, judgment, or the need to have everything figured out before you arrive.

Clients often come to me during seasons of anxiety, grief, burnout, relationship strain, or transition. Together, we’ll slow down what feels overwhelming, clarify what matters, and build practical ways to move through life with more steadiness and self-trust.

My approach

Practical support with room for your whole story.

Therapy is not about being told what to do. It’s a collaborative process of understanding patterns, strengthening resources, and creating change that fits your real life.

Collaborative

We’ll work together to identify what’s keeping you stuck and what helps you feel more steady, connected, and clear.

Trauma-informed

Sessions prioritize safety, choice, pacing, and nervous system awareness so the work can feel supportive rather than overwhelming.

Grounded

Alongside insight, we’ll build practical skills for boundaries, communication, self-compassion, and emotional regulation.
